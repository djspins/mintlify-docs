## Search Quality

Coco uses advanced search capabilities to provide you with:

- **Real-time results** - Information is current and up-to-date
- **Relevant sources** - Results come from reliable and authoritative websites
- **Comprehensive answers** - Multiple sources are consulted to give you complete information
- **Contextual understanding** - Coco understands the intent behind your questions

<Note>
Internet search results are based on publicly available information. For sensitive topics like health or financial advice, always consult with qualified professionals.
</Note>

## Tips for Better Search Results

To get the most relevant information from Coco's internet search:

- **Be specific** - "Italian restaurants in downtown Boston" instead of just "restaurants"
- **Include location** - When asking about local businesses or weather, specify the city or area
- **Use current terms** - "Today's weather" or "current stock price" for time-sensitive information
- **Ask follow-up questions** - Coco can provide more detailed information if you need clarification

## Privacy and Search

Your search queries are processed to provide you with the best results, but Coco respects your privacy:

- Search queries are not stored permanently
- Personal information is not shared with external search services
- Results are provided directly through WhatsApp for your convenience

<Warning>
While Coco strives to provide accurate information, always verify important facts from official sources, especially for critical decisions.
</Warning>
